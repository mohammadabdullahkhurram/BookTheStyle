# BookTheStyle launch film — VO script

~78s at natural pace. Audience: salon owners. Tone: warm, direct, a little dry. Not hype.

[0:00–0:14 — Cold open] It's 2pm on a Tuesday. Your best chair is empty. The call came in at 11 last night — after you'd closed, after you'd stopped answering. So it went to voicemail. And she booked somewhere else.

[0:14–0:20 — Logo promise] BookTheStyle. Your chairs, booked. Even at 11pm.

[0:20–0:36 — Her side] She picks the service. Her stylist. A time that works. Thirty seconds, from her phone, while your lights are off. No call. No wait. No maybe-I'll-try-later.

[0:36–0:50 — Your side] And it's just… there in the morning. On your calendar, in her profile. No-shows handled before you've had your coffee. The week fills itself in.

[0:50–1:02 — Make it yours] And it looks like you. One color, and the whole thing is yours — your brand, your booking page, your salon. Not ours.

[1:02–1:12 — Proof] Bookings taken while you slept. No-shows, down. One line of code to go live.

[1:12–1:18 — Close] BookTheStyle. Fill every chair. bookthestyle.com
