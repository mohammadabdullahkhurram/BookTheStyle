Widget designs

Twenty takes on the booking widget, full flow each.

Every design shows the complete embed flow — service, stylist, date and time, details, confirmation — as five step cards. Each card is rendered at 300px, the width the widget occupies on a phone, so what you see IS the mobile experience; embedded on desktop the card simply centres in its container. Scroll a row horizontally to walk the flow. All designs are light except 15 · Nocturne (dark elegant), the deliberate dark option. Step cards render at the exact width the widget occupies on a phone — the mockups ARE the mobile experience.
